UI/UX Design

UI/UX design refers to the combined practice of designing both the user interface (UI) and the user experience (UX) of digital products. UI design focuses on the visual aspects of an interface, including the layout, color schemes, typography, and interactive elements, ensuring that they are aesthetically pleasing and intuitive to use. UX design, on the other hand, is concerned with the overall experience of the user, encompassing the ease of use, efficiency, and satisfaction with the product.
